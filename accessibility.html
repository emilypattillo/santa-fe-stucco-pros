Accessibility

This site should work for everyone — including people using screen readers, keyboard navigation, magnification, or voice control. That is the standard it is built and measured against.

What the site is built to support

The site aims for WCAG 2.1 Level AA: readable contrast throughout, visible keyboard focus on every link, button, and form field, touch targets sized for real thumbs, text that scales with browser zoom, descriptive alt text on photographs, and labeled forms that screen readers announce correctly. Motion is minimal and reduced-motion preferences are respected.

If something gets in your way

If any part of this site is hard to use with your setup, please say so — call (505) 416-4951 or use the contact form and describe what happened and what browser or assistive technology you were using. Issues get fixed, and you are welcome to handle the whole request by phone if the site is the obstacle.

Ongoing work

Accessibility is maintained, not achieved once — pages get checked when they change, and this statement gets updated as the site does.
