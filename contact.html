Helpful to mention (if you know it)

None of this is required — it just sharpens the first visit:

• What you are seeing — cracks, stains, soft spots, fade, or a project (addition, re-stucco)
• Which walls or where on the house
• Whether the home might be real adobe or sits in a historic district
• Your area — Santa Fe, Eldorado, Las Campanas, Tesuque, Los Alamos, Española, or nearby

If all you know is “the stucco looks rough,” send exactly that.

What happens next

A callback confirms your area and what is going on, then a look-at-it visit reads the walls — material, condition, parapets, the cause behind any damage — and produces an itemized quote before work starts. Pre-winter crack and parapet sealing gets seasonal priority; nothing proceeds, and nothing expands, without the conversation happening first. Confirm licensing, insurance, and scope directly before any company starts work.
